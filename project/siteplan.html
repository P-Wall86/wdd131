WDD 131 - PROJECT SITE PLAN

Site Name

The site name is "York English Academy". The name reflects the academy's passion and commitment to teaching English, combining tradition (since 1986) with a clear focus on language education. Optional domain availability: yorkacademy.com.ar

Site Purpose

York English Academy is dedicated to providing high-quality English education focused on student success and achievement. Our website serves as a central hub for students, parents, and educators, offering detailed information about our teaching methods, important dates, and leisure activities designed to enhance learning. We also provide clear guidance on CEFR levels, the benefits of international exams, and how our programs prepare students for real-world language proficiency. At York, we prioritize transparency, communication, and excellence, ensuring both students and parents are informed and engaged throughout the learning journey.

Scenarios

"My child is struggling with English at school. How can York English Academy help them improve, and what makes your teaching methods different from other institutes?"

"I want to study abroad. How can York English Academy help me prepare for the language proficiency test?"

Color Schema

The primary color, Dark Navy (#031b2c), is used for headers and footers. The secondary color, Light Blue (#f0f4f8), serves as the background for the main content. Accent color Golden Yellow (#FFC632) is used for buttons and highlights. Finally, the Light Gray (#EBEBEB) color for fonts ensures readability on darker backgrounds. These colors aligns with the academy's brand identity.

Typography

Bevan Normal is used for headings and subtitles. Cutive Normal is used for body text. Both fonts, along with the color schema, align with the brand logo.
