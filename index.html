About me

My name is Pamela, but my friends and family call me Pam. I was born in Misiones, Argentina, and have lived here all my life. I currently work as a private English tutor, specializing in teaching students with dyslexia. In my free time, I love playing instruments, especially the guitar and piano. I also enjoy taking long walks along the promenade and sharing mate with my friends.

Misiones, Argentina

Misiones is one of the 24 provinces of Argentina, located in the northeastern part of the country. The official flag features the colors red, blue, and white, which symbolize the blood shed for independence, the commitment to being part of the Republic, and the province's distinction and greatness.
